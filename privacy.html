Privacy Policy

This app (“Activity”) is a personal project that uses your Facebook data only for the specific purpose of deleting or moderating content that you own. No data is shared with third parties, and all requests are performed on‑demand with a valid Facebook access token.

What is collected

Only data that is explicitly requested by the user’s access token. (Posts, photos, videos, events, etc.) No other data is accessed or stored.

How we use the data

The data is used solely to execute the deletion operation you initiate. It is never stored, logged, or persisted beyond the lifetime of the request.
