How to Delete Your Data from 'Activity'

We respect your privacy. If you wish to delete all data that 'Activity' has stored on your behalf, and to remove any content you previously requested us to delete from Facebook, simply follow the steps below.

Step 1 – Navigate to Data Deletion Page

Open the following link in a browser (you can bookmark it):

Data Deletion Page

Step 2 – Confirmation Prompt

Click the button below. The page will ask you to confirm one‑time action. Once confirmed, the app will:

  • Remove any records that belong to you in our local database (if you store them). (In this personal‑project app there are none.)
  • Invoke the Facebook Graph API delete calls you triggered. They are performed on‑demand and do not require a separate deletion operation here.
  • Send you a final “deletion complete” email (if you provided an e‑mail address) – otherwise a public confirmation message appears.

Step 3 – Done

After you confirm, you will immediately see “Your data has been deleted.” No further action is required.

Need Help?

If you hit an error or were unable to delete, please email us at teslations@gmail.com or visit our GitHub issues page.
